My Soccer Journey

Soccer has been a big part of my life since elementary school. I first played for my school’s soccer team, and after moviplng to the U.S., I joined Fay School Varsity Soccer team, where I played for three years and served as captain for two. During the off-season, I played with the Boston Bolts U15 and NEFC U16, competing in league matches and tournaments. This year, I joined The Hotchkiss School and am part of the Varsity Soccer program here. Soccer has taught me the value of teamwork and I plan to keep playing and chellenging myself at higher levels.
